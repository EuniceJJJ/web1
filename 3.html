Emma Watson

Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson (born 15 April 1990) is an English actress and model.
By the age of ten, Watson had performed in various Stagecoach productions and school plays, including Arthur: The Young Years and The Happy Prince, but she had never acted professionally before the Harry Potter series.
Following the Dragon School, Watson moved on to Headington School, near Oxford
